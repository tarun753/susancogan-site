FAQ

Common questions

What's the difference between coaching and therapy?

Thapy focuses on healing the past. Coaching focuses on moving forward. I'm not a therapist — I'm trained to ask questions that help you discover your own answers and build the future you want.

What happens in the free session?

We'll talk about what's on your mind, what you'd like to change, and whether coaching is right for you. No pressure, no sales pitch. Just a real conversation. Most people leave with at least one new insight.

How does sliding scale pricing work?

You choose what you pay within the $50-$150 range based on what feels right for your situation. I trust you to pick a number that works. No questions asked, no judgment.

Do you offer virtual sessions?

Yes! I coach clients across the country via phone and video call. Location is never a barrier. Many of my longest-standing clients I've never met in person.

What if coaching isn't for me?

That's exactly why the first session is free. You'll know after 30 minutes whether this is right for you. If it's not, I'll help you find what is — no hard feelings, ever.
